Crockpot Italian Chicken

Description

You only need three ingredients for this delicious dinner meal. It is one of my favorite meals to make during the week because it's so simple and ready to eat as soon as I get home!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 (16 oz.) bottle of Italian dressing
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Stir the Italian dressing and Parmesan cheese together in a bowl.
  2. Place the chicken in the crockpot, and pour the dressing mixture over the chicken.
  3. Cover and cook on low for about 8 hours until chicken is tender and the juices run clear.
